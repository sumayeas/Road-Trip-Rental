How to Purchase a Car?

Follow these simple steps to purchase your metro car:
  • Visit the “Purchase car” section from the homepage > Services > Purchase car.
  • Select your departure station “From” and destination station “To”.
  • Select the date and time of your travel.
  • Choose the number of cars you wish to purchase.
● Click on the “Purchase” button to proceed to payment.
Once you’ve made your payment, your car will be confirmed and your journey is set!
